# 🧱 Visibrick

> Your complete LEGO® collection companion.

Visibrick is a modern cross-platform application designed for LEGO collectors who want to catalog, organize, value, and identify every part of their collection. Whether you're tracking thousands of sets, individual minifigures, or loose pieces, Visibrick is built to make managing your collection simple and enjoyable.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📚 Complete Collection Tracking

* Track owned LEGO sets
* Track individual minifigures
* Track loose parts and bricks
* View collection statistics
* Organize by themes, years, colors, and more

### 🧩 Piece-Level Inventory

Know exactly what you own.

Visibrick stores inventories down to the individual piece level, allowing you to:

* View every part contained in a set
* Track missing pieces
* See duplicate parts across your collection
* Search your inventory instantly

### 💰 Price Tracking

Stay on top of your collection's value.

Track both **New** and **Used** market prices over time, including:

* Collection value
* Individual set values
* Minifigure values
* Historical pricing trends
* Gain/Loss tracking

### 📷 AI-Powered Identification *(Coming Soon)*

One of Visibrick's biggest features is instant identification using your device's camera.

Simply point your camera at:

* A LEGO set
* A minifigure
* Individual LEGO parts

Visibrick will identify what you're looking at and pull up detailed information about it.

No more scrolling through catalogs trying to figure out what that mysterious minifigure came from.

### 🔍 Powerful Search

Quickly search by:

* Set number
* Set name
* Minifigure ID
* Part number
* Theme
* Color
* Year
* Piece count

### ☁️ Cloud Sync *(Planned)*

Keep your collection available everywhere.

* Secure account syncing
* Multiple device support
* Automatic backups
* Fast synchronization

## 🎯 Vision

Visibrick aims to become the ultimate LEGO collector's toolkit.

Instead of juggling spreadsheets, multiple websites, and pricing tools, everything you need will be available in one application.

Whether you're:

* A casual collector
* A reseller
* A BrickLink store owner
* A completionist
* A lifelong LEGO fan

Visibrick is designed to help you understand and manage your collection better than ever before.
